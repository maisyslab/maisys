Current Projects

  • Ribcage Implant Reconstruction: Developing deep learning models to generate 3D implants from CT scans for rib cage reconstruction.
  • Automated Rib Fracture Detection: Creating machine learning models for the detection, localization, and characterization of traumatic rib fractures in CT scans.
  • Multi-Modal Cancer Survival Prediction: Integrating diverse data types to improve the accuracy of survival outcome predictions in cancer diagnosis.
